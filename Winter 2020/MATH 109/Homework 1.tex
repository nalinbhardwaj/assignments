\section*{Problem 1.43}
	For $r \in \mathbb{R^+}, A_r = \{x \in \mathbb{R} : |x| < r\}$ \\
	
	Since for all $n \in \mathbb{R}$, there exists $r \in \mathbb{R^+}$ such that $r > |n| \implies n \in A_r \implies \bigcup_{r \in \mathbb{R^+}} A_r = \mathbb{R}$ \\ \\
	Since $\lim_{r \to 0} A_r = \{0\},  \bigcap_{r \in \mathbb{R^+}} A_r = \{0\}$
